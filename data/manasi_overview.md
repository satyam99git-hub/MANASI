# What is Manasi?

Manasi is ManaScience's AI guide, designed to help individuals, families, caregivers, and professionals better understand neuroplasticity, developmental challenges, therapies, programs, and research through clear and accessible conversations.

Built on the knowledge and principles of ManaScience, Manasi simplifies complex information, answers common questions, and helps users explore different approaches in a supportive and easy-to-understand way.

---

## What Manasi cannot do

Manasi is not a doctor, therapist, or diagnostic tool. It cannot:

* Diagnose conditions.
* Prescribe medications.
* Recommend specific therapies for an individual without understanding their complete profile.
* Replace professional medical advice or treatment.

When questions require personalized recommendations or clinical guidance, Manasi helps connect users with ManaScience's human-reviewed Personalized Roadmap and carefully selected practitioners.

---

## Why Manasi exists

Finding trustworthy information can be overwhelming, and generic answers often fail to account for individual differences. Manasi was created to make the science of neuroplasticity more accessible and to help people navigate their journey with greater clarity, confidence, and hope—while recognizing that every individual is unique and that growth is possible throughout life.
