# ManaScience – Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## What is ManaScience?

ManaScience is an educational and guidance platform rooted in the science of neuroplasticity. It helps individuals and families better understand developmental, neurological, learning, sensory, and cognitive challenges through clear, accessible information and personalized support.

ManaScience brings together simplified research, therapies, programs, and practitioner access in one place, making it easier for people to navigate their unique journey. Rather than focusing only on diagnoses, ManaScience recognizes that every individual has different strengths, challenges, and goals, and that support should be tailored to the person.

### The platform offers:

* Educational content on neuroplasticity, conditions, therapies, and programs.
* Research Digest, which translates scientific findings into easy-to-understand language.
* Therapy and program libraries that help users explore different approaches.
* A Personalized Roadmap, which provides human-reviewed guidance based on an individual's history, strengths, and goals.
* Access to carefully selected practitioners, currently focused in India with plans to expand globally.
* A community space where families and individuals can learn from shared experiences.

ManaScience does not diagnose conditions, prescribe treatments, or replace medical professionals. Its mission is to make the science of neuroplasticity accessible and help people discover personalized pathways for growth, learning, and lifelong development.

---

## Is ManaScience a hospital or a therapy centre?

No. ManaScience is neither a hospital nor a therapy centre. It is a guidance platform rooted in the science of neuroplasticity. ManaScience helps individuals and families understand conditions, explore therapies and programs, access simplified research, and connect with carefully selected practitioners through personalized roadmaps.

## Who made ManaScience? Are they doctors?

**NOT SURE**

ManaScience was founded by Devyani Kanodia, who has spent years studying neuroplasticity and understanding the challenges families face in finding the right support. ManaScience brings together scientific knowledge and human expertise, but it is not a medical institution and does not claim to replace doctors or healthcare professionals.

## Is ManaScience safe to use for my child?

Yes. ManaScience is designed to be a safe and supportive educational resource for families. It does not diagnose conditions, prescribe medications, or recommend treatments without understanding the individual. Instead, it provides evidence-informed information and personalized guidance reviewed by a real human team and qualified practitioners.

---

## Is ManaScience only for autism, or does it support other conditions too?

No. ManaScience supports individuals with a wide range of developmental, neurological, learning, sensory, and cognitive challenges. This includes autism, ADHD, dyslexia, developmental delays, speech and language difficulties, sensory processing challenges, motor coordination issues, anxiety, and many other conditions. ManaScience also supports parents, caregivers, educators, and adults seeking guidance with relevant curated programmes.

---

## How is ManaScience different from just Googling my child's condition?

Searching online often leads to overwhelming, conflicting, and fragmented information. ManaScience brings trusted knowledge together in one place and explains it in simple, accessible language. More importantly, it recognizes that two individuals with the same diagnosis may have very different strengths, challenges, and needs. Instead of offering one-size-fits-all answers, ManaScience helps families understand the bigger picture and provides access to personalized guidance and carefully selected practitioners when needed.

---

## How is ManaScience different from platforms like Understood.org or Brain Balance?

ManaScience combines education, research, and personalized guidance within a single ecosystem. While many platforms focus on providing information or promoting specific programs, ManaScience recognizes that no single approach works for everyone. It brings together diverse therapies, programs, and research through the lens of neuroplasticity and helps families access carefully selected practitioners. The focus is not on a diagnosis or a single solution, but on understanding the individual and creating a personalized path forward.

---

## What qualifications do the practitioners on the platform hold?

Practitioners on ManaScience are carefully selected through a human review process. The team evaluates their professional background, training, experience, areas of expertise, and alignment with the values of individualized care. Depending on their field, practitioners may include occupational therapists, speech-language pathologists, psychologists, neurodevelopment specialists, functional medicine practitioners, educators, and other trained professionals. ManaScience prioritizes quality, experience, and a commitment to personalized support.

Rather than offering one-size-fits-all answers, ManaScience combines scientific understanding, human expertise, and carefully selected practitioners to help families make more informed decisions with confidence and hope.

## Is ManaScience safe for adults as well as families?

Yes. ManaScience is designed to support individuals across the lifespan, including children, teenagers, adults, and the families and caregivers who support them. The platform provides educational information and personalized guidance rooted in neuroplasticity, while recognizing that every person's journey is unique. ManaScience does not replace medical care or provide diagnoses, making it a safe and supportive resource for learning and navigating next steps.

---

## How personalized are the recommendations?

Personalization is at the heart of ManaScience. Rather than relying solely on a diagnosis, recommendations are shaped by an individual's strengths, challenges, developmental history, goals, and circumstances. Because no two people are the same, ManaScience focuses on understanding the whole person and creating a pathway that reflects their unique needs instead of offering one-size-fits-all solutions.

---

## How do you decide what support to suggest?

ManaScience combines insights from neuroscience, research, and practitioner expertise with information shared by the individual or family. Through the Personalized Roadmap process, the ManaScience team reviews factors such as strengths, challenges, goals, and history before connecting individuals with appropriate resources and practitioners. Support is guided by the belief that meaningful outcomes come from individualized understanding rather than generalized recommendations.

## What makes ManaScience different from generic mental health or self-help sites?

Most mental health and self-help platforms offer broad advice designed for large audiences. ManaScience takes a different approach. It is built around the science of neuroplasticity and recognizes that people with similar diagnoses or challenges can have very different needs. Instead of focusing on quick fixes or generic content, ManaScience combines simplified research, diverse therapies and programs, practitioner access, and personalized guidance to help individuals and families discover support that is tailored to them. Its goal is not simply to provide information, but to help people navigate a clearer and more individualized path toward growth and development.
