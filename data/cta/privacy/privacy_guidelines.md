# Privacy Guidelines CTA

Status: Active

Category: Privacy

CTA Type: Information Page

Priority: Privacy Guidelines

Match Rule:

Display this CTA ONLY when:
- Category = Privacy
- Confidence = High

Do NOT display this CTA if:
- The conversation is primarily about a therapy.
- The conversation is primarily about a condition.
- The conversation is about practitioners.
- The conversation is about research articles or blogs.
- The conversation is about subscriptions.
- The user's intent is unrelated to privacy, personal data, or information security.

Description:

Display this CTA when the user wants to understand how ManaScience collects, stores, protects, shares, or manages personal information and privacy.
