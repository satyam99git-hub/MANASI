# What Therapies Does ManaScience Cover?

ManaScience brings together a diverse range of therapies and approaches through the lens of neuroplasticity and individualized support. Rather than promoting a single solution, ManaScience helps individuals and families understand different approaches and connect with carefully selected practitioners based on their unique needs.

---

# Primitive Reflexes

Primitive reflexes are automatic movement patterns present from birth that support early development. When these reflexes remain active beyond infancy, they may influence movement, learning, attention, and sensory processing.

### MNRI® (Masgutova Neurosensorimotor Reflex Integration)

MNRI® (Masgutova Neurosensorimotor Reflex Integration) supports the integration of reflex patterns to improve motor, sensory, emotional, and cognitive functioning.

### Feldenkrais Method®

Feldenkrais Method® uses gentle movement and awareness to enhance coordination, posture, and nervous system organization.

---

# Cognition

Cognitive approaches focus on strengthening learning, attention, memory, reasoning, and processing skills.

### Arrowsmith Program®

Arrowsmith Program® uses targeted cognitive exercises based on neuroplasticity principles to strengthen specific brain functions.

### Jill Stowell Learning Centers®

Jill Stowell Learning Centers® provide individualized educational interventions to improve reading, writing, comprehension, and learning skills.

---

# Brain Waves

These approaches focus on self-regulation, attention, and optimizing brain function.

### Neurofeedback

Neurofeedback is a non-invasive method that provides real-time information about brainwave activity to support attention and emotional regulation.

### Access Consciousness®

Access Consciousness® is a holistic approach aimed at promoting greater awareness and mental well-being.

---

# Vision

Vision involves not only how clearly we see, but also how the brain processes visual information.

### Vision Therapy

Vision Therapy helps strengthen visual skills such as eye tracking, focusing, teaming, and visual processing. ManaScience includes approaches such as NeuroVision and the work of Dr. Mueller.

### Lynn Valley Optometry®

Lynn Valley Optometry® offers developmental and behavioral vision approaches that support visual efficiency and functional vision.

---

# Brain & Gut Health

These approaches recognize the close relationship between overall health, the nervous system, and brain function.

### Nemechek Protocol®

Nemechek Protocol® focuses on gut-brain health and autonomic nervous system regulation through nutritional support.

### Cellular Hydration

Cellular Hydration aims to optimize cellular health and physiological function.

### Ayurveda

Ayurveda is a traditional system of health that emphasizes balance through individualized diet, lifestyle, and natural practices.

### Naturopathy

Naturopathy supports the body's natural healing processes through nutrition and holistic wellness approaches.

---

# Listening

Listening and auditory interventions support communication, sensory integration, and nervous system regulation.

### Safe and Sound Protocol® (SSP)

Safe and Sound Protocol® (SSP) uses specially filtered music to promote nervous system regulation and social engagement.

### Tomatis® Method

Tomatis® Method uses sound-based stimulation to support listening, attention, communication, and sensory processing.

### Integrated Listening Therapy

Integrated Listening Therapy combines auditory stimulation with movement and sensory experiences to support brain-body integration.

---

# The ManaScience Approach

ManaScience does not provide these therapies directly. Instead, it helps individuals and families:

* Understand different therapies and the areas they support.
* Explore the science and principles behind various approaches.
* Learn from research and practitioner expertise.
* Connect with carefully selected practitioners.
* Receive personalized guidance through a human-reviewed Personalized Roadmap.

Because every individual is unique, ManaScience believes that meaningful progress comes from understanding the whole person—not from applying the same therapy to everyone.
